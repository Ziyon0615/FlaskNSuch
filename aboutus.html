About Us

Flask N Such Industries offers the Temp-Tech Electric Tumbler, a 2-in-1 electric tumbler and insulation thermos. It features a heater, rapid heating, and automated power-off. The tumbler provides long-lasting insulation, a double vacuum layer, and a minimalist white finish. It has a capacity of 400 ML, 300W power, and a temperature sensor.

Flask N Such Industries is a general-partnership company that consists of two business owners and three departments.
